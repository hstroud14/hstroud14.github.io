Pitch Up! Competition

This April, Hannah founded the Pitch Up! Competition throught the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Texas A&M. The competition encouraged students to build their presentation skills and perfect their "elevator pitch" for their research or other technical project.

Hannah based this competition off the widely popular 3 Minute Thesis Competition and adapted this model for an undergraduate participation pool. Students presented their work in non-technical terms in 3 minutes or less to a panel of judges comprised of both field experts and individuals without a technical background.

Hannah coordinated, marketed, found funding for, and developed judging criteria for this competition. In its first iteration, the competition had seven participants. Hannah hopes to continue the competition in coming years in the department and eventually, with the help of her advisor, to scale it up to the whole college of engineering.
